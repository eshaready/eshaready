Open Catsket

This project, my capstone, was created in 6 weeks with a group of 5 people using C# and Unity. I developed much of the code around the cat tether, death/destroying and key/unlock mechanics, the menu and level select screens, the special platforms, and helped contribute to level design. I also created many of the in-game assets (with a partner creating the other assets), including all the environmental objects, all the backgrounds (including the parallaxing of them), the logo, and the ghost cat sprites, and the code for animating most objects.

antigrav

Controls: A/D or arrow keys to move; SPACE to jump; SHIFT to flip gravity; ESC for menu

This project was created in 1.5 weeks using C# and Unity. I developed this entire game alone; I created the UI, all the gameplay, and the sprite art. I used free assets for font, background image, and sound; these are credited in the itch.io link.

NES Legend of Zelda: Recreation + Custom Level

Controls: WASD or arrow keys for movement. X/Z for weapons. Space to switch between alt weapons. Press 4 for the custom level.

This project was created using C# and Unity. Along with my partner, I recreated the first dungeon of the NES LoZ game, and added a custom level introducing a laser mechanic. I implemented functionality for the sword, bow, and boomerang weapons that Link uses and handled enemy movement and logic. In our custom level, I laid out the new level rooms & helped handle laser target, magic door, and enemy spawning functionality, and experimented with sprites.
